Our project is on Gujarat Tourism


We have divided all tourist places of Gujarat into 7 categories: Holy Places,
Historical Legacies (Heritage Places), Stunning Oceanfronts (Beaches),
Essence of Biosphere and Fauna (Zoo, National parks, etc.),
Incredible Treasuries (Museums), Special Attractions and Beautiful Cities.
We have provided information, photos, how to reach, near by places and near by hotels of particular places.
We also have provided login and registration facility to user.
User can also give feedback of our website and also give comments about places.
